# AI Job Trend Analysis (2025–2030): Emerging Opportunities and Workforce Impact  
Using Excel, SQL, and Power BI

## Project Overview
This project analyzes and forecasts **AI-related job trends from 2025 to 2030**, with a strong focus on **how AI adoption is creating new job opportunities** across industries.

The objective is to identify **industry-wise, location-wise, and job title-wise job openings**, while understanding how AI-driven transformation leads to the **emergence of new roles, evolving skill requirements, and employment growth**, rather than only job displacement.

---

## Tools & Technologies
*Excel**
  - Data cleaning and preprocessing
  - Pivot Tables for summarizing job roles, skills, and trends
  - Conditional Formatting to highlight demand patterns and growth areas
- **SQL**
  - Querying AI job data
  - Aggregation and trend analysis by role, location, and impact level
- **Power BI**
  - Interactive dashboards
  - Visualizations showing emerging AI job opportunities and future trends


---

## Analysis Focus
The analysis is centered on:
- **Industry-wise job opportunity growth**
- **Location-wise expansion of AI-related roles**
- **Job title-wise emergence of new and evolving positions**
- **AI Impact Levels (Low, Moderate, High)** and their influence on job creation
- **Forecast period: 2025–2030**

Key metrics include projected job openings, job growth percentage, automation risk, remote work trends, salary patterns, and experience requirements.

---

## Key Insights
- Identified emerging AI job roles likely to create new employment opportunities
- Highlighted high-growth skills and roles using Excel Pivot Tables
- Used conditional formatting to visually emphasize demand spikes and trend changes
- Analyzed how AI adoption is creating new job categories rather than only replacing roles
- Presented future-focused insights through Power BI dashboards

---

## Files Included
- Excel datasets used for job market analysis and forecasting  
- SQL queries for extracting and analyzing projected job trends  
- Power BI dashboard file showcasing interactive insights into **future job opportunities**  

---

## Outcome
This project provides a forward-looking, data-driven view of how AI is expected to **create new job opportunities between 2025 and 2030**.  
The insights support **career planning, workforce reskilling, and hiring strategies**, helping individuals and organizations prepare for an AI-driven job market.
